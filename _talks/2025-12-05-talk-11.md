<p style="text-align:justify">The eCIR is a fully practical exam, which immerses candidates in a realistic lab environment simulating a corporate breach. Examinees must investigate the incident and answer a series of scenario-based questions that mirror the type of findings and information typically included in a professional incident report.</p>
